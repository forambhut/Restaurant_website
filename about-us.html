About Us

Since 2015 Gourmet Traditional Restaurant is serving authentic foods an meals, Multi Cuisine dishes and a vast variety of food options to the people of Gujarat. We are the fastest growing food chain that now has its branches in Vadodara, Surat, and Valsad. We are profoundly known for our Signature Gujarati Thali having an exquisite variety of dishes for you. Visit us for a memorable food experience.

Our Best Chefs

Shipra Khanna

The winner of MasterChef India Season 2, Ms. Shipra Khanna’s alluring charm as a Prolific Chef, Global Television Personality, Consultant and a social worker is distinctive. Awe inspiring the world with the bravura of her impeccable talent, she’s exalted herself to a show stealer.

Sanjay Thumma

Sanjay Thumma popularly known as Vah-Chef is an Indian chef and founder of the cooking website, vahrehvah.com. He is especially popular among the Indian expatriate communities in mainland Europe, Australia and North America.

Tarla Dalal

Tarla Dalal was an Indian food writer, chef, cookbook author and host of cooking shows. Her first cook book, The Pleasures of Vegetarian Cooking, was published in 1974. Since then, she wrote over 100 books and sold more than 10 million copies.

Ranveer Brar

In a landmark move, Italy’s Bertolli Olive Oil has appointed leading culinary expert and Celebrity Chef Ranveer Brar as their first Indian brand ambassador for its olive oil portfolio in Indian shores today. A global market leader in olive oil, Bertolli is owned by Deoleo, the biggest olive oil company in the world.
